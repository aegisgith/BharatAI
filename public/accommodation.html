About the Location

All featured hotels are strategically located in South Mumbai's premium neighborhoods - Cuffe Parade, Colaba, Nariman Point, and Marine Drive. These areas offer:

  • Walking distance or short drive to World Trade Center Mumbai
  • Easy access to Mumbai's business district and tourist attractions
  • Proximity to Gateway of India, Marine Drive promenade, and Colaba Causeway
  • Well-connected to Mumbai International Airport (30-40 minutes)
  • Abundant dining, shopping, and entertainment options
