Conference Overview

India is at a decisive moment in AI. The IndiaAI Mission has committed ₹20,000 crore to compute, talent, and research. AWS is deploying $8.3 billion in Maharashtra infrastructure. Government ministers are chairing AI policy committees. The rules for India's AI decade are being written now — not in 2030.

This conference is where those decisions get debated in public: by the policymakers who write the rules, the researchers who build the models, the CXOs deploying them at scale, and the founders staking their companies on the outcome.

Keynotes
Visionary addresses from global AI leaders, ministers, and tech executives setting the agenda for India's next chapter
Panels
Multi-perspective debates on AI policy, ethics, investment, and industry transformation — moderated for substance, not soundbites
Workshops
Hands-on sessions on GenAI, Agentic AI, LLM fine-tuning, and AI product development led by practitioners
Research
Peer-reviewed paper presentations from India's top AI researchers in academia and industry
Innovation Talk
8-minute spotlight on the Innovation Stage — direct access to policymakers, investors, and enterprise leaders in the room

10+ Specialised Tracks

Five parallel halls, each built for a specific professional audience. Navigate to yours.

Generative AI & LLMs
Large language models, multimodal AI, RAG, and AI agents. For: ML engineers, AI product managers, and GenAI teams building at scale.
AI for Governance
Digital India, smart city platforms, and public sector AI. For: government officials, smart-city programme heads, and public sector CIOs.
Healthcare & Life Sciences AI
Medical diagnosis, drug discovery, telemedicine, and clinical workflows. For: hospital CIOs, pharma tech leaders, and healthtech founders.
Manufacturing & Industry 4.0
Predictive maintenance, quality control, and smart factory systems. For: plant heads, manufacturing CIOs, and Industry 4.0 technology teams.
Agricultural AI
Precision farming, crop prediction, and soil analytics. For: agritech founders, agriculture ministry officials, and rural tech innovators.
FinTech & BFSI AI
Digital payments, fraud detection, and algorithmic trading. For: CIOs and CTOs in banking, insurance, and capital markets.
AI in Education
Personalized learning, assessment AI, and EdTech platforms. For: EdTech founders, university leaders, and corporate L&D heads.
AI Ethics & Responsible AI
Bias mitigation, explainability, and privacy-preserving AI. For: AI ethics researchers, legal teams, and policy advisors.
Mobility & Smart Transport
Autonomous vehicles, traffic management, and logistics AI. For: transport ministry officials, logistics CTOs, and mobility startups.
