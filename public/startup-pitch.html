Eligibility Criteria

Is your startup ready for the Innovation Stage?

AI-First
Building AI-powered products, services, or solutions — AI as a core component, not a feature add-on
Stage
Seed to Series A — demonstrable traction, live MVP, or early product-market fit evidence
India
Registered Indian entity, or significant market operations and focus within India
Differentiation
Clear, specific differentiation from existing solutions — a novel approach, not a feature clone
Business model
Viable path to revenue with a scalable unit economics thesis — not just a technology demo
Team
Founding team with relevant domain expertise, full-time commitment, and skin in the game
