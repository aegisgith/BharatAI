Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 May 2026

Aegis Knowledge Trust ("we", "us", "our"), the organiser of the Bharat AI Innovation Conference & Exhibition 2026 ("the Event"), is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Indian Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and applicable data-protection laws.

1. Data we collect

  • Registration data: name, email, phone, organisation, job title, industry, company size, city, country, special requirements, package selected.
  • Communication data: messages you send via our contact forms, email, or phone.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device, pages visited, referrer, collected via standard server logs and analytics cookies.
  • Photographs and recordings: we may photograph or record sessions and the exhibition floor for promotional use.

2. Purpose of processing

  • To process your registration, issue passes, and provide event access.
  • To send event-related operational communications (schedule, venue, badging, networking app).
  • To send the post-event newsletter and information about future Aegis events (only if you opt in).
  • To improve our website and event experience.
  • To comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

3. Sharing

We share data only with: (a) trusted service providers (payment processor, email delivery, networking-app vendor, ticketing) under written confidentiality terms; (b) sponsors and partners only with your explicit consent through opt-in actions you take in the networking app; (c) regulators or law enforcement when required by law.

We do not sell personal data.

4. International transfers

Some service providers operate outside India. We use providers with industry-standard security and contractual safeguards.

5. Retention

Registration data is retained for up to 3 years after the Event for accounting and follow-up. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data, withdraw consent, and lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India. To exercise these rights email info@bharataiinnovation.com.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Security

We use HTTPS, access controls, and reasonable security measures. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify you of any breach affecting your data as required by law.

9. Children

The Event is intended for adults aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Updates

We may update this Policy. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
