Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 8 May 2026

This policy applies to all paid passes for Bharat AI Innovation Conference & Exhibition 2026.

1. Pass transfers

You may transfer your pass to a colleague at no charge up to 7 days before the Event. Email the new attendee’s name, email, phone, organisation, and job title to info@bharataiinnovation.com.

2. Refund schedule

  • More than 30 days before the Event: 100% refund, less a ₹500 administrative fee.
  • 15 to 30 days before the Event: 50% refund.
  • Less than 15 days before the Event: No refund. Pass transfer remains available up to 7 days before the Event.

3. How to request a refund

Email info@bharataiinnovation.com with subject line "Refund Request — [your registration reference]". Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 14 business days of approval.

4. Non-refundable items

  • Booth packages and sponsorship fees (governed by your signed Exhibitor or Sponsor Agreement).
  • Travel, accommodation, and visa costs incurred by you or your delegates.
  • Convenience fees, payment-gateway fees, and bank charges.

5. If we cancel or postpone the Event

If the Event is cancelled by us for reasons other than force majeure, you will receive a full refund of pass fees paid.

If the Event is postponed, your pass will automatically be valid for the rescheduled dates. If you cannot attend the new dates, a full refund will be available within 30 days of the postponement notice.

If the Event is moved to a virtual format due to force majeure, a partial refund of up to 50% of the pass fee will be offered, recognising that virtual attendance still delivers significant content value.

6. Disputes

Any refund disputes will be resolved in accordance with our Terms of Service.
